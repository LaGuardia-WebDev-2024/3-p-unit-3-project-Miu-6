Recipe "Lemonade"

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves:
Ingredients Quantity
lemons 3-4
Sugar 1 cup
water 1 cup

Step 1: Combine 1 cup each of sugar and water and cook over medium heat until the sugar dissolves.

Step 2: Set the syrup aside to cool for about 10 minutes.

Step 3: Combine the cooled syrup and freshly-squeezed lemon juice in a pitcher.

Step 4: Add the remaining water and stir, then pour over ice and enjoy!
